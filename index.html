Why Choose Tile Roofing?

Tile roofing has become increasingly popular among homeowners for several reasons:

  • Durability: With proper maintenance, tile roofs can last over 50 years. They resist rot, fire, and insects, making them a long-term investment.
  • Aesthetic Appeal: Tile roofs come in various styles, colors, and shapes. This versatility can enhance your home’s beauty and increase its market value.
  • Energy Efficiency: Tile roofs reflect sunlight, keeping your home cooler during hot summers. This feature can lead to lower energy bills.
  • Low Maintenance: Compared to other roofing materials, tile roofs require less maintenance, making them a practical choice for busy homeowners.

How to Find Certified Tile Roofing Contractors

Finding reliable certified roofing contractors can seem daunting. Here are some steps to help you in your search:

1. Ask for Recommendations

Start by asking friends, family, or neighbors for recommendations. If someone you trust has had a positive experience with a roofing contractor, it’s likely that you will too.

2. Research Online

Use the internet to research local certified tile roofing contractors. Websites like Titan Roofing AZ offer comprehensive information about their services and customer reviews. Look for reviews and ratings on platforms like Google and Yelp.

3. Check Credentials

Once you have a list of potential contractors, check their credentials. Ensure that they are certified and licensed to work in your area. You can usually find this information on their websites or by contacting them directly.

4. Request Quotes

Contact several contractors to request quotes for your project. Make sure to provide the same information to each contractor so you can compare their offers fairly. Look for transparency in their pricing and whether they include all necessary details in their quotes.

5. Ask About Experience

Inquire about the contractor’s experience, specifically with tile roofing. Ask for examples of previous projects and if possible, visit some of their completed jobs. This will give you an idea of their workmanship and attention to detail.

6. Review Contracts Carefully

Before signing a contract, review it carefully. Ensure it includes details about the work to be done, materials to be used, payment schedule, and warranty information. If anything is unclear, don’t hesitate to ask questions.

7. Check References

Request references from the contractor and follow up with them. Ask about their experience with the contractor, the quality of work, and whether they would recommend them. This step can provide valuable insights into the contractor’s reliability.

Common Tile Roofing Problems and Solutions

Even with a high-quality tile roof, issues may arise over time. Here are some common problems and how certified contractors can address them:

Cracked or Broken Tiles

Tiles can crack or break due to severe weather or other impacts. Certified contractors can replace damaged tiles quickly, restoring the integrity of your roof.

Leaks

Leaks can occur if tiles are not installed properly or if they have shifted over time. Certified contractors can identify the source of leaks and make necessary repairs to prevent further damage.

Poor Installation

Improperly installed tile roofs can lead to various issues, including water intrusion and reduced lifespan. Certified contractors have the skills to correct installation mistakes and ensure that your roof is secure.

Mold and Algae Growth

In humid climates, mold and algae can develop on tile roofs. Certified contractors can clean the roof and apply treatments to prevent future growth, keeping your roof looking its best.
